Dr. Jacques Janse van Rensburg, Clinical Haematologist, has been registered with the Department of Health as well as the South African Oncology Consortium (SAOC) for 3 years to mix and administer chemotherapy for Haematological conditions at his practice in Mediclinic Vergelegen, Somerset West.

He has been at Vergelegen since 2014 and is also active in hospital and practice as a Specialist Physician. He is assisted by a registered oncology nurse, Sr Lidia Marais, who has 18 years of experience in the administering and monitoring of chemotherapy infusions.

There is a brand new 6-treatment-station unit (5 chairs and one bed) in the new Block One at Vergelegen and treatment is offered at medical aid tariffs (all schemes).

Haematological conditions most likely to benefit from the new unit include, but not limited to, Multiple Myeloma, Lymphoma, Chronic leukaemia’s and Bone marrow failure syndromes.
